In nature, dead trees are just as normal as live trees. If you've been in Alaska less than a year you're a Cheechako. It's cold, but it's beautiful. Let the paint work. We want to use a lot pressure while using no pressure at all.

Just go out and talk to a tree. Make friends with it. Use what happens naturally, don't fight it. God gave you this gift of imagination. Use it.
